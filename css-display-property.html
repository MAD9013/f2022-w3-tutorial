Things to keep in mind...

Inline

Inline elements can't receive widths, will break between lines, and their padding and margin spill outside of their box. display: inline; is best used for adding styles to text within a paragraph because it stays within the flow of text.

Inline-Block

Inline-block elements automatically get a bit of spacing around them that you can't remove. Meaning four inline-block elements that are 25% width each, will actually take up more than 100% of the space.
